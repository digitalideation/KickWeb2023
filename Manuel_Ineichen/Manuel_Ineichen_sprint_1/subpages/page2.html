Usage

CUSTREQ is used in the following manner, ass- uming a continuous line:

CustReq "hailtext" "text" "pos" -"neg" [-d default] [Ft timeout] [-x xpos] [-y ypos]

  • hailtext - the title text of the custom requester
  • text - the main text of the custom requester
  • pos - tjhe positive option of the custom requeter
  • neg - the regative option od the custom requester.
  • default - the optional default value, either 1 (yes) or 0 (no).
  • timeout - the optional timeout value in seconds.
  • xpos - the optional x position of the window.
  • ypos - the optional y position of the window.
